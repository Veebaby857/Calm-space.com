Why it works

Your brain relaxes when it senses safety. Steady sounds like rainfall or ocean waves mask jarring noise and have no sudden changes, so your nervous system can downshift. It's the same reason we feel calm in nature.

Ready to try it? Below are four core soundscapes you can play right now. Each one is looped seamlessly, so there are no gaps or jarring restarts. Pick what fits your mood, hit play, and let the background fade away.
